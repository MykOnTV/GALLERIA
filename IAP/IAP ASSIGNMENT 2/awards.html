Bill Gates

Biography Awards Contributions

Bill Gates has won several awards:

  • He achieved the National Medal of Technology & Innovation in 1992.
  • In 1994, he was awarded the Distinguished Fellow of the British Computer Society award.
  • He also achieved the Satellite Special Achievement Award for Outstanding Contribution to Entertainment New Media in 1997.
  • He was awarded the Silver Buffalo in 2010.
  • In 2013, the Bambi Millennium Award was given to Gates. Also, the Lasker-Bloomberg Public Service Award was given to him in the same year.
  • Bill Gates was awarded the Padma Bhushan in 2015.
  • In 2016, Bill Gates achieved the Presidential Medal of Freedom.
